FAQs

Q: So how does it work?

Remember the last time your weekly meeting started with a clear agenda, ran like clockwork, and wrapped up early with everyone knowing exactly what to do next? Can’t remember? That’s why we built Meetly.

Q: What exactly does MeetMate do?

MeetMate makes your meetings suck less. We cut out the pointless ones and make the necessary ones razor-sharp. You’ll walk in with a clear agenda, know exactly who’s saying what, and leave with actionable steps—no confusion, no wasted time.

Q: How does MeetMate save me time?

By making every minute count. We help you ditch redundant meetings, streamline the ones that matter, and ensure no one’s wondering, “Why am I here?” Instead, you’ll spend less time in meetings and more time actually getting work done.

Q: What makes MeetMate different from other tools?

Most tools just help you schedule meetings. We help you fix them. With MeetMate, you’re not just booking a slot; you’re walking into a productive session where everyone’s prepped, and you’re out the door before you know it—with a plan in hand.

Q: How does MeetMate handle follow-ups?

We track follow-ups automatically, so no more “Wait, who was supposed to do that?” After the meeting, you’ll have a clear list of tasks and owners. Think of it like having a virtual meeting secretary—minus the coffee runs.

Q: Why does my team need MeetMate?

Because meetings don’t have to be soul-sucking. With MeetMate, your team will spend less time talking in circles and more time hitting goals. Imagine wrapping up a meeting and thinking, “Damn, that was productive.”

Q: What’s MeetMate’s pricing model?

Simple and straightforward—just like your meetings will be. MeetMate offers tiered pricing based on the size of your team, so whether you're a lean startup or a growing business, you’ll only pay for what you need. Plus, no surprise fees. More productivity, zero bullshit.
